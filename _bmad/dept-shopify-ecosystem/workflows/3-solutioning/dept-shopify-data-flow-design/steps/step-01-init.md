# Step 1: Data Flow Analysis Kickoff

## Objective

Understand data flow scenario, source/destination systems, and business requirements.

## Instructions

Gather requirements:
- What specific data is being synced? (products, customers, orders, etc.)
- Which system is source? Which is destination?
- Business rules or transformations applied?
- How often does data need to sync?
- What is expected volume and performance target?
- Any data quality or compliance requirements?

Review data models:
- Source system: What data is available? What fields? What are the constraints?
- Destination system: What data can it accept? What fields are required?
- Are there any data type mismatches?

Document assumptions and constraints.

## Outputs

- Data flow scenario documented
- Source/destination systems identified
- Data models reviewed
