# Step 5: Go-Live Plan & Final Sign-Off

## Objective

Produce final go-live plan document and obtain executive sign-off.

## Instructions

Create go-live plan document including:

1. **Executive Summary**
   - Go-live date/time
   - Expected duration
   - Business impact (downtime, user impact)
   - Key milestones

2. **Readiness Summary**
   - Validation results
   - Implementation status
   - Training completion
   - Open issues and mitigations

3. **Go/No-Go Decision**
   - Checklist results
   - Any conditional items and status
   - Final decision: Go | Conditional Go | No-Go

4. **Cutover Plan**
   - Phase-by-phase schedule
   - Activities and owners
   - Timing and dependencies
   - Communication plan

5. **Rollback Strategy**
   - Decision criteria
   - Procedures per platform
   - Time limits
   - Responsible parties

6. **Support & Monitoring**
   - On-call team structure
   - Monitoring dashboards and alerts
   - Escalation procedures
   - Support hotline info

7. **Sign-Offs**
   - Business owner: _____ Date: _____
   - Technical lead: _____ Date: _____
   - Operations: _____ Date: _____
   - Steering committee: _____ Date: _____

## Outputs

- Final go-live plan document (15-20 pages)
- Executive summary (2 pages)
- Signed/approved by all stakeholders

## Completion Criteria

- Plan complete and comprehensive
- All sign-offs obtained
- Team briefed and ready
- Ready to execute go-live
