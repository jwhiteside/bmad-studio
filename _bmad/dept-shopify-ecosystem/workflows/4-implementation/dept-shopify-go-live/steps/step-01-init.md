# Step 1: Go-Live Preparation

## Objective

Gather readiness information and confirm all prerequisites for go-live.

## Instructions

Gather completion status:
- Validation reports: All integrations pass/conditional/fail?
- Implementation status: All platforms configured? All integrations built?
- User training: Completion percentage, any gaps?
- Documentation: All runbooks and training materials ready?
- Support readiness: On-call team trained? Escalation procedures ready?
- Business readiness: Go-live team ready? Business users prepared?

Identify any open issues or risks:
- Are there critical issues preventing go-live?
- Are there workarounds or mitigations in place?
- What contingencies are in place?

Confirm final go-live date/time and window.

## Inputs

- Validation reports
- Implementation status
- Training completion reports
- Readiness assessment from teams

## Outputs

- Go-live readiness summary
- Open issues and mitigations
- Go-live date/time confirmed
