# Workflow: Shopify Ecosystem Go-Live

## Overview

Final go/no-go assessment and go-live preparation. Validate readiness, plan cutover, and execute ecosystem launch.

## Prerequisites

- All validations completed with pass/fail status
- Implementation activities complete
- Training complete
- Rollback procedures planned
- Go-live schedule confirmed

## Steps

1. **Go-Live Preparation**: [step-01-init](steps/step-01-init.md)
2. **Go/No-Go Checklist**: [step-02-checklist](steps/step-02-checklist.md)
3. **Cutover Planning**: [step-03-cutover](steps/step-03-cutover.md)
4. **Rollback Strategy**: [step-04-rollback](steps/step-04-rollback.md)
5. **Go-Live Plan**: [step-05-launch](steps/step-05-launch.md)

## Completion Criteria

- All go/no-go criteria met or mitigated
- Cutover sequence clear and communicated
- Rollback procedures tested and documented
- Team trained and ready
- Executive sign-off on go-live plan
- Ready for launch
