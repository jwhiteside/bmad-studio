# Shopify Platform Audit

**Entry Point**: `dept-shopify-platform-audit`

Comprehensive health assessment of existing Shopify ecosystem. Evaluates integration reliability, identifies anti-patterns that create technical debt, reviews cost vs. value delivered, and produces prioritized improvement recommendations.

## What This Skill Does

- Documents current platform stack and integration landscape
- Assesses integration health (sync success rates, reliability, error frequency)
- Systematically checks for 6 anti-patterns
- Reviews platform costs and ROI
- Produces audit report with prioritized optimization recommendations

## When To Use It

- Periodic health assessment of existing ecosystem (annual or bi-annual)
- Investigating performance issues or integration failures
- Evaluating whether ecosystem still serves business needs
- Preparing for ecosystem expansion or modernization
- Cost review and optimization

## Inputs

- Current platform inventory and configuration
- Integration logs and performance data (if available)
- Platform licensing and cost documentation
- Team feedback on pain points and friction

## Outputs

- Platform audit report (markdown) with:
  - Platform inventory and satisfaction assessment
  - Integration health scorecard
  - Anti-pattern findings and severity
  - Cost analysis and optimization opportunities
  - Prioritized recommendations with estimated impact
