# Workflow: Shopify Platform Audit

## Overview

Comprehensive health assessment of existing Shopify ecosystem. Evaluates each platform, integration reliability, anti-patterns, costs, and produces prioritized recommendations for optimization and improvement.

## Prerequisites

- Access to platform documentation and inventory
- Integration logs or team knowledge of sync reliability
- Current vendor contracts and licensing documentation
- Team available for interviews on pain points

## Steps

1. **Platform Inventory & Assessment**: [step-01-init](steps/step-01-init.md)
2. **Integration Health Assessment**: [step-02-integration-health](steps/step-02-integration-health.md)
3. **Anti-Pattern Analysis**: [step-03-anti-patterns](steps/step-03-anti-patterns.md)
4. **Cost Review & Optimization**: [step-04-cost-review](steps/step-04-cost-review.md)
5. **Audit Report & Recommendations**: [step-05-report](steps/step-05-report.md)

## Completion Criteria

- All platforms in use documented with current status
- Integration health assessed for each integration
- All 6 anti-patterns systematically evaluated
- Cost analysis identifies optimization opportunities
- Audit report includes overall health score
- Recommendations are specific, quantified, and prioritized
- Executive and technical teams validate findings
