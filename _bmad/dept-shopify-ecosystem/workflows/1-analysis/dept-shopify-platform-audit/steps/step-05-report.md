### Report Attributes

- Length: 8-12 pages typical
- Tone: Objective, data-driven, constructive
- Format: Professional document suitable for executive review
- Appendices: Detailed data tables, integration maps, cost models

## Inputs

- Platform inventory and assessment (Step 1)
- Integration health scorecard (Step 2)
- Anti-pattern analysis (Step 3)
- Cost analysis and opportunities (Step 4)
- Overall business context and objectives

## Outputs

- Comprehensive Platform Audit Report (8-12 pages)
- Executive Summary (1-2 pages)
- Prioritized Action Plan with timeline
- Health scorecard and success metrics
- Implementation roadmap

## Completion Criteria

- Audit report complete and comprehensive
- Overall health score calculated and justified
- Top issues clearly identified with impact quantified
- Recommendations are specific, prioritized, and actionable
- Implementation timeline realistic
- Financial impact (savings/investment) modeled
- Report reviewed and validated by operations and technical leadership
- Stakeholders signed off on findings and recommendations
- Ready to proceed to implementation planning
