# Step 1: Platform Inventory & Current Assessment

## Objective

Document complete current platform inventory with versions, licensing, user counts, and satisfaction levels. Establish baseline for health assessment.

## Instructions

Create inventory of all platforms in current Shopify ecosystem:

### For Each Platform

**Platform Identification**:
- Platform name and vendor
- Category (ecommerce, CRM, PIM, analytics, marketing, fulfillment, etc.)
- Version/edition currently deployed
- Go-live date and tenure in stack

**Usage & Ownership**:
- Primary business function (why do we use this)
- Internal user count
- Internal owner/team responsible
- Vendor contact and relationship quality (1-5 rating)

**Financial**:
- Annual license cost
- Professional services costs (annual estimate)
- User seat costs (if applicable)
- Integration/API licensing (if separate)
- Estimated total annual cost for this platform

**Data & Performance**:
- Data volume (customers, records, transactions per month if applicable)
- System uptime (if available)
- Performance issues noted (if any)
- Data quality issues (if any)

**Satisfaction & Health**:
- Team satisfaction score (1-5, where 5 = very satisfied)
- Known pain points (list 2-3 key frustrations)
- Unmet requirements (what business needs aren't being met)
- Likelihood of renewal at contract expiration (1-5, where 5 = definitely renew)

### Platform Summary Table

Create table with all platforms:

| Platform | Category | Version | Users | Annual Cost | Satisfaction | Pain Points |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Shopify | Ecommerce | Plus | 12 | $2,300/mo | 4/5 | Limited PIM capabilities |

### Overall Ecosystem Statistics

Calculate from inventory:
- Total platforms: X
- Total annual cost: $X
- Average platform satisfaction: X/5
- Cost per user across ecosystem: $X
- Cost per dollar of revenue: X%

## Inputs

- Current platform contracts and licenses
- IT system documentation
- Team interviews on platform usage

## Outputs

- Complete platform inventory with all details
- Platform summary table
- Overall ecosystem statistics

## Completion Criteria

- All active platforms documented
- Licensing and cost information current (within last 30 days)
- User counts accurate
- Team satisfaction scores validated
- No platforms missed
