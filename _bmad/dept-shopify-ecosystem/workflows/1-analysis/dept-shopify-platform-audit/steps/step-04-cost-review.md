### Value Assessment

For each platform, assess cost vs. value:

| Platform | Annual Cost | Value Rating (1-5) | Cost/Value | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Platform A | $X | 4 | Good | Renew |
| Platform B | $X | 2 | Poor | Consolidate or retire |

**Value Rating Scale**:
- 5: Essential platform, high team satisfaction, generates clear ROI
- 4: Important platform, good satisfaction, justified cost
- 3: Adequate platform, acceptable satisfaction, cost is fair
- 2: Low value, team has frustrations, cost seems high
- 1: Critical issues, high dissatisfaction, cost unjustified

## Inputs

- Platform costs (from Step 1)
- Platform satisfaction scores (from Step 1)
- Vendor contracts and licensing details
- Team feedback on which platforms deliver value

## Outputs

- Cost consolidation and analysis
- Value assessment for each platform
- Identified optimization opportunities with estimated savings
- Cost optimization roadmap
- Target state costs and savings

## Completion Criteria

- Total ecosystem cost documented
- Each platform assessed for cost vs. value
- Optimization opportunities identified and quantified
- Annual savings from all opportunities calculated
- Implementation timeline and effort estimated
- 3-year financial impact modeled
- CFO/Finance lead validates cost analysis
