# Step 1: Business Context Gathering

## Objective

Gather comprehensive business profile information needed to match the business to an appropriate ecosystem pattern. This step establishes the baseline context for all subsequent analysis.

## Instructions

Systematically collect the following business profile information. If any information is not available, note it as "TBD" and flag for collection before moving to pattern matching.

### Revenue & Scale
- Annual revenue (current and projected 3-year)
- Revenue growth rate (YoY percentage)
- Gross margin percentage
- Primary revenue source (DTC, wholesale, hybrid)
- Geographic revenue distribution (top markets)

### Product Portfolio
- Total SKU count (current and projected)
- Variant depth (avg variants per base product)
- Product categories (breadth)
- Physical vs digital products ratio
- Complexity of product data requirements (description, specs, variants, bundles, etc.)

### Sales Channels
- Primary sales channels (Shopify store, marketplace, wholesale, B2B, subscription, etc.)
- Channel breakdown (% of revenue per channel)
- Multi-brand or multi-store structure
- Channel-specific requirements (different product assortments, pricing, data needs)

### Geographic Scope
- Number of active markets
- Localization requirements (language, currency, tax, shipping)
- Local data residency or regulatory requirements (GDPR, etc.)
- Global expansion plans (new markets in next 12-24 months)

### Organizational & Team
- Team size (ecommerce, product, operations, marketing)
- Technical capability level (internal vs agency reliance)
- Decision-making structure (centralized vs federated)
- Technology budget and constraints

### Business Context & Priorities
- Primary business objectives (growth, margin, customer experience, market expansion)
- Main pain points with current platform setup (if applicable)
- Strategic initiatives (personalization, content marketing, subscription, B2B launch, etc.)
- Time-to-value expectations (quick wins vs long-term build)

## Inputs

- Business stakeholder input
- Financial documentation or estimates
- Current operational metrics (if available)

## Outputs

- Completed business profile document with all sections filled
- Flagged items marked TBD with collection deadline

## Completion Criteria

- Business profile document is at least 80% complete (TBD items documented and tracked)
- All major business characteristics captured
- No material gaps that would prevent pattern matching
- Team alignment on accuracy of documented profile
