# Step 4: Pattern Matching

## Objective

Match the business profile to the most appropriate ecosystem pattern. Evaluate which of 6 established patterns best fits the business characteristics and priorities.

## Instructions

Review the business profile from Step 1 and evaluate against each ecosystem pattern. For each pattern, assess fit across multiple dimensions.

### Pattern 1: Starter Plus

**Characteristics**:
- $0-2M revenue, typically early-stage
- 100-1000 SKUs
- Single market (or early expansion)
- Minimal team, high technical savvy
- Focus on getting to PMF and growth

**Platform Stack**:
- Shopify Plus as ecommerce core
- Klaviyo or simple email
- Minimal integrations
- GA4 for analytics

**Fit Evaluation**:
- Does business match revenue/SKU scale?
- Is single-market or early-expansion?
- Is team lean with technical capability?
- Are integration needs minimal?

### Pattern 2: Content-Rich

**Characteristics**:
- Content is primary driver of traffic and conversion
- Significant SEO and organic traffic focus
- Editorial calendar and team
- May have multiple content types (blog, video, guides)
- Medium revenue ($2-20M range typical)

**Platform Stack**:
- Shopify Plus for ecommerce
- Headless CMS (Contentful, Sanity, or similar)
- Content management tooling
- SEO tools (SEMrush, Ahrefs)
- Rich media management
- Email/CDP for nurturing content readers

**Fit Evaluation**:
- Is content a primary business driver?
- Is there significant SEO/organic focus?
- Does business support editorial staff?
- Are integration needs around content management?

### Pattern 3: Global Enterprise

**Characteristics**:
- Multi-market presence (5+ countries typical)
- Multi-currency, multi-language
- High regulatory/compliance requirements (GDPR, data residency)
- Large team with decentralized decision-making
- Revenue typically $20M+

**Platform Stack**:
- Shopify Plus with multi-market configuration
- Central PIM for product data consistency
- Localization middleware
- Advanced CDP (Segment, mParticle)
- Regional fulfillment tools
- Global compliance/audit tools

**Fit Evaluation**:
- Is business truly global with 5+ markets?
- Are localization and regulatory requirements complex?
- Is there decentralized team structure?
- Is revenue scale and complexity sufficient?

### Pattern 4: Subscription-First

**Characteristics**:
- Subscription revenue is primary (50%+ of revenue)
- Recurring billing and customer lifecycle management critical
- Complex subscription tiers or products
- Retention and churn management primary focus

**Platform Stack**:
- Shopify Plus with subscription apps (Bold, ReCharge, etc.)
- Dedicated subscription management platform
- Advanced CDP for lifecycle management
- Dunning/billing recovery tools
- Churn analytics and prediction

**Fit Evaluation**:
- Is subscription 50%+ of revenue?
- Is customer lifetime management critical?
- Are there complex subscription tiers?
- Is retention management a primary focus?

### Pattern 5: D2C Growth

**Characteristics**:
- High growth trajectory (40%+ YoY)
- Heavy marketing spend (channels: paid social, affiliate, influencer)
- Data-driven marketing and optimization
- Rapid experimentation culture
- Typically $5-50M revenue range

**Platform Stack**:
- Shopify Plus for platform agility
- Advanced CDP (Segment, Tealium)
- Marketing automation (Klaviyo, HubSpot)
- Analytics and BI (Looker, Tableau)
- Multiple ad platform connectors
- A/B testing frameworks

**Fit Evaluation**:
- Is growth trajectory 40%+ YoY?
- Is marketing spend significant and multi-channel?
- Is culture data-driven and experimental?
- Are revenue and team size in expected range?

### Pattern 6: Headless Commerce

**Characteristics**:
- Custom storefront critical for differentiation
- API-first architecture requirement
- Technical team capable of custom development
- Non-traditional commerce scenarios (mobile-first, embedded, OmniChannel)
- Often paired with another pattern

**Platform Stack**:
- Shopify Plus or Commerce Cloud with headless mode
- Dedicated frontend framework (Next.js, Vue, etc.)
- API gateway and orchestration
- Advanced personalization engine
- Custom integration layer

**Fit Evaluation**:
- Is custom storefront a competitive requirement?
- Is there internal development capability?
- Are non-traditional commerce scenarios present?
- Does business need API-first flexibility?

## Matching Methodology

For each pattern:
1. Score fit across key dimensions (1-5 scale)
2. Identify strengths (where business aligns with pattern)
3. Identify mismatches (where business doesn't align)
4. Calculate overall fit score (weighted average)

**Dimensions to evaluate**:
- Revenue scale (1-5)
- Product/SKU complexity (1-5)
- Geographic scope (1-5)
- Team structure (1-5)
- Technical capability (1-5)
- Primary business drivers (1-5)
- Regulatory/compliance needs (1-5)

## Decision Framework

1. Identify pattern(s) with fit score 4+ (strong match)
2. If multiple strong matches, evaluate trade-offs:
   - Which pattern aligns with business priorities?
   - Which pattern allows for future evolution?
   - Which pattern minimizes cost and complexity?
3. Select primary pattern and note secondary patterns (if applicable)
4. Document reasoning for pattern selection

## Inputs

- Business profile (from Step 1)
- Current stack and anti-pattern analysis (from Steps 2-3)

## Outputs

- Pattern matching analysis document with:
  - Fit scores for all 6 patterns
  - Detailed reasoning for top 2-3 patterns
  - Recommended pattern with justification
  - Evolution path (how business might migrate between patterns as it grows)

## Completion Criteria

- All 6 patterns evaluated with fit scores
- Recommended pattern identified
- Reasoning document explains why chosen pattern fits business
- Key pattern characteristics validated with business stakeholders
- Evolution path considered (how will pattern need to change in 3-5 years)
