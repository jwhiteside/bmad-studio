# Workflow: Shopify Ecosystem Assessment

## Overview

Comprehensive assessment of a Shopify ecosystem (existing or proposed) that evaluates business profile, documents platform composition, identifies anti-patterns that create technical debt, matches to ecosystem patterns, and produces prioritized improvement recommendations.

## Prerequisites

- Business profile documentation (revenue, growth, SKUs, channels, geographies, team)
- Access to current platform inventory (if assessing existing ecosystem)
- Understanding of business strategic priorities and constraints

## Steps

1. **Business Context Gathering**: [step-01-init](steps/step-01-init.md)
2. **Current Stack Documentation**: [step-02-current-stack](steps/step-02-current-stack.md)
3. **Anti-Pattern Analysis**: [step-03-anti-pattern-check](steps/step-03-anti-pattern-check.md)
4. **Pattern Matching**: [step-04-pattern-match](steps/step-04-pattern-match.md)
5. **Gap Analysis**: [step-05-gap-analysis](steps/step-05-gap-analysis.md)
6. **Recommendations & Report**: [step-06-recommend](steps/step-06-recommend.md)

## Completion Criteria

- All business profile questions answered or documented
- Current stack fully inventoried (if applicable)
- All 6 anti-patterns systematically evaluated
- Recommended ecosystem pattern justified with business reasoning
- Gap analysis identifies specific platforms/integrations to add or remove
- Report includes prioritized recommendations with implementation sequencing
- Assessment signed off by strategy lead and technical architect
