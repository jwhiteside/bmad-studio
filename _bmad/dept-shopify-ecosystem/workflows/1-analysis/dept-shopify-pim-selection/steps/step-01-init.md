# Step 1: Product Context Gathering

## Objective

Gather comprehensive product portfolio data and governance requirements needed to assess PIM complexity and platform requirements.

## Instructions

Collect the following information about product data:

### Product Portfolio Overview

- Current total SKU count
- Product categories/hierarchy depth
- Typical product attributes (how many attributes per product; examples: title, description, images, specs, dimensions, weight, etc.)
- Variant structure (how many variants per base product on average; example: size + color = 10 variants)
- Digital vs. physical product mix

### Product Data Characteristics

- Product data enrichment level (basic: name/desc only; intermediate: specs/images/pricing; advanced: rich media, videos, 3D models)
- Product images per SKU (average number and file sizes)
- Digital asset requirements (videos, 3D models, interactive content)
- Rich media needs (needs video, 360 views, AR, etc.)
- Product data quality issues (incomplete descriptions, missing images, inconsistent formatting)

### Channel & Localization Needs

- Number of sales channels selling products (Shopify, marketplace, B2B, wholesale)
- Geographic markets served (countries, languages, currencies)
- Channel-specific product assortments (do all products sell in all channels?)
- Localization requirements (product descriptions in multiple languages, regional specs)
- Pricing by region/channel

### Data Governance & Workflow

- Who owns product data (single team or decentralized)?
- Product data approval workflow (is there a review/approval process?)
- Data quality standards and validation rules
- Compliance requirements (accessibility, data privacy, ingredient labeling, certifications)
- How often product data changes (seasonality, continuous updates, batch changes)
- Product data creation process (internal, supplier uploads, automated enrichment)

### Current State (if applicable)

- Where is product data currently stored (Shopify only, spreadsheets, existing PIM, etc.)?
- Data silos or sources of truth conflicts
- Pain points with current product data management
- Frequency of data sync errors or manual fixes

### Team & Resources

- Size of product/merchandising team managing product data
- Technical capability for data import/management
- Budget available for PIM licensing and implementation
- Timeline expectations (how soon do you need PIM live?)

## Inputs

- Stakeholder interviews (product, ecommerce, merchandising teams)
- Current product data samples
- Existing spreadsheets or data documentation

## Outputs

- Product portfolio profile document with all sections completed
- Sample of current product data (structure and content)
- Current data challenges and priorities documented

## Completion Criteria

- All product portfolio data collected
- Team agrees on product data characteristics
- Current challenges and priorities clear
- Ready to proceed to complexity scoring
