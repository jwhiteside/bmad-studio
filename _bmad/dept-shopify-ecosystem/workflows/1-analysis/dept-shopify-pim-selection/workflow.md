# Workflow: Shopify PIM Selection

## Overview

Structured decision guide for selecting product information management (PIM) platform for Shopify ecosystem. Uses complexity scoring to evaluate whether Akeneo, Bluestone, Inriver, or no dedicated PIM approach is most appropriate.

## Prerequisites

- Product portfolio data (SKU count, variants, channels)
- Understanding of data governance requirements
- Budget and timeline constraints documented
- Stakeholder alignment on decision criteria

## Steps

1. **Product Context Gathering**: [step-01-init](steps/step-01-init.md)
2. **Complexity Scoring**: [step-02-complexity-score](steps/step-02-complexity-score.md)
3. **Platform Evaluation**: [step-03-evaluate](steps/step-03-evaluate.md)
4. **Integration Assessment**: [step-04-integration-assess](steps/step-04-integration-assess.md)
5. **Selection & Recommendation**: [step-05-recommend](steps/step-05-recommend.md)

## Completion Criteria

- Product portfolio data fully documented
- PIM complexity score calculated and validated
- All four approaches (Akeneo, Bluestone, Inriver, No-PIM) evaluated
- Recommendation clearly justified with scorecard
- Integration requirements mapped
- Implementation timeline and costs estimated
- Stakeholder sign-off on selected platform
