# Step 1: Review & Architecture Kickoff

## Objective

Review ecosystem assessment and platform selections. Establish architecture team, confirm scope, and set up for detailed architecture design work.

## Instructions

### Review Ecosystem Assessment

- Confirm recommended ecosystem pattern (Starter Plus, Content-Rich, Global Enterprise, Subscription-First, D2C Growth, Headless Commerce)
- Review platform selections (Shopify Plus edition, PIM selection, CRM, CDP, fulfillment, etc.)
- Confirm business drivers and integration priorities
- Note any constraints or special requirements

### Establish Architecture Team

Identify and confirm participation of:
- Enterprise Architect (owns overall architecture decisions)
- Data Architect (owns data architecture and flows)
- Technical Lead for Integration (owns integration patterns)
- Shopify Solutions Architect (if managed by vendor)
- Business Requirements Owner (confirms alignment with business)
- Security/Compliance Lead (validates security architecture)

### Scope Definition

Define architecture scope:
- What systems will be integrated? (List all)
- What data flows are in scope? (Current integrations + new ones)
- What timeframe? (Design for growth over 3-5 years)
- What SLAs and reliability requirements? (Uptime %, latency targets, etc.)
- What constraints? (Budget, timeline, technical, regulatory, etc.)

### Architecture Assumptions

Document key assumptions:
- Customer identity will be mastered in [system]
- Product data will be mastered in [system]
- Order data will be mastered in [system]
- Real-time vs. batch sync expectations
- Multi-language and multi-market requirements
- Channel-specific requirements

### Architecture Review Points

Schedule review and sign-off gates:
- Architecture pattern selection review: [Date]
- Data architecture design review: [Date]
- Data flow specification review: [Date]
- ADR review and approval: [Date]
- Final architecture document sign-off: [Date]

## Inputs

- Completed ecosystem assessment document
- Platform selection recommendations
- Business requirements and integration priorities
- Organization chart and decision authorities

## Outputs

- Architecture scope statement
- Architecture team roster
- Key assumptions documented
- Review and sign-off schedule

## Completion Criteria

- Architecture team identified and committed
- Scope clearly defined and agreed
- Key assumptions documented and validated
- Review schedule established
- Ready to begin pattern selection
