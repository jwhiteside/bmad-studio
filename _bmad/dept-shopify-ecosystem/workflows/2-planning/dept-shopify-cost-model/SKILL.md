# Shopify Ecosystem Cost Model

**Entry Point**: `dept-shopify-cost-model`

Build comprehensive cost model for Shopify ecosystem implementation and operation. Includes platform licensing, integration implementation, ongoing operational costs, and total cost of ownership analysis.

## What This Skill Does

- Estimates per-platform monthly licensing costs
- Estimates integration implementation costs (labor, middleware, custom dev)
- Estimates ongoing operational costs (support, maintenance, monitoring)
- Calculates total 3-year cost of ownership
- Models ROI and payback period
- Identifies cost optimization opportunities

## When To Use It

- After platform selections are finalized
- For business case and financial approval
- Budget planning and cost control
- Vendor negotiation preparation

## Inputs

- Platform selections with vendor information
- Architecture and integration plan
- Estimated implementation timeline
- Team size and cost assumptions

## Outputs

- Detailed cost model with:
  - Platform licensing costs (monthly and annual)
  - Implementation costs by phase
  - Ongoing operational costs
  - 3-year total cost of ownership
  - ROI analysis and payback period
  - Cost by business function
  - Sensitivity analysis
