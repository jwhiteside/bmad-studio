# Step 2: Platform Licensing Costs

## Objective

Calculate monthly and annual recurring platform licensing costs.

## Instructions

For each platform, document:

**Per-Platform Cost Structure**:

| Cost Component | Unit | Quantity | Unit Cost | Monthly | Annual |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Base license | Month | 1 | $X | $X | $X |
| Per-user seat | Seat | N | $X | $X | $X |
| Per-SKU/record | SKU | N | $X | $X | $X |
| API calls | 1M calls | N | $X | $X | $X |
| Data storage | GB | N | $X | $X | $X |
| **Total per platform** | | | | | **$X** |

Document any:
- Volume discounts (multi-year commits)
- Growth fees (increases as business scales)
- Premium features or editions
- Support tier costs

**Year 1, Year 2, Year 3 Projections**:
- Show how costs change as business scales
- Include price increase assumptions (2-3% annually typical)

Calculate ecosystem total licensing cost for years 1-3.

## Outputs

- Per-platform licensing cost breakdown
- Year 1-3 licensing costs
- Total recurring monthly/annual cost

## Completion Criteria

- All platform costs documented
- Multi-year view included
- Vendor pricing validated
