## Objective

Synthesize all costs into total cost of ownership model and ROI analysis.

## Instructions

Create cost model showing:

**Year-by-Year Cost Breakdown** (3-year typical):

| Cost Category | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Licensing (recurring)** | $X | $X | $X |
| **Implementation (one-time)** | $X | $0 | $0 |
| **Operations (recurring)** | $X | $X | $X |
| **Total Annual Cost** | **$X** | **$X** | **$X** |

Calculate:
- **3-Year Total Cost of Ownership**: $X (sum of years 1-3)
- **Average Annual Cost**: $X (TCO / 3)
- **Cost per $1M Revenue**: $X
- **Cost per Transaction**: $X (if applicable)

**ROI & Payback Analysis**:

Identify business value from implementation:
- Time savings: X hours/month × $X/hour = $X/year
- Error reduction: Reduce manual data entry errors from X to Y = $X/year value
- Faster time-to-market: Reduce product launch cycle from X weeks to Y = $X/year value
- Improved conversion: Better personalization from [data insight] = $X/year revenue lift
- Reduced support costs: Better data quality = X fewer support tickets = $X/year

**Total Annual Business Value**: $X/year

**ROI Calculation**:
- (Annual Value - Annual Cost) / Investment = ROI %
- Payback Period: Years to recover initial investment

**Sensitivity Analysis**:

Show how costs/ROI change if:
- Platform volume 20% higher than expected
- Implementation takes 20% longer
- Business value realization delayed 6 months

**Optimization Opportunities**:

Identify ways to reduce cost:
- Multi-year licensing commitments (save X%)
- Consolidate vendors where possible (save $X/yr)
- Use managed services vs. internal staff (cost comparison)
- Optimize platform selection (save $X/yr)

## Outputs

- 3-year cost model with breakdown
- TCO calculation
- ROI and payback analysis
- Sensitivity scenarios
- Cost optimization recommendations

- All costs included and realistic
