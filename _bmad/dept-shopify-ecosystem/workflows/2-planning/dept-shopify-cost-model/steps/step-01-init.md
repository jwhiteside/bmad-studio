# Step 1: Cost Model Kickoff

## Objective

Gather platform information, confirm cost assumptions, and establish cost model framework.

## Instructions

- Collect vendor pricing from all platform selections (Shopify, PIM, CRM, CDP, fulfillment, etc.)
- Get vendor quote/estimates for expected volume/scale
- Confirm any bundled pricing, multi-year discounts
- Get implementation effort estimates from vendors/partners
- Confirm internal team costs (loaded rates)
- Define cost categories: licensing, implementation, operations

## Inputs

- Platform selections with vendor contact information
- Business scale (revenue, orders, products, customers)
- Implementation plan timeline

## Outputs

- Vendor pricing data collected
- Cost assumptions documented
- Cost model structure defined

## Completion Criteria

- Pricing for all platforms collected
- Implementation estimates received
- Cost structure defined
