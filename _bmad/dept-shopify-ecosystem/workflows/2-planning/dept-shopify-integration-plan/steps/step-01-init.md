# Step 1: Plan Kickoff & Review

## Objective

Establish planning team, review architecture and business context, and set planning scope.

## Instructions

- Confirm planning team (Program Manager, Project Manager, Technical Lead, Business Owner)
- Review architecture document and platform selections
- Confirm business timeline expectations and critical path items
- Establish planning assumptions (resource availability, team structure)
- Define what constitutes "go-live" (all platforms live, all integrations tested, user trained)

## Inputs

- Architecture design document
- Platform selections and vendor information
- Business requirements and priorities

## Outputs

- Planning team roster and responsibilities
- Planning assumptions documented
- Go-live success criteria defined

## Completion Criteria

- Team identified and committed
- Assumptions validated
- Scope and success criteria clear
