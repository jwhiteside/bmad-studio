# Workflow: Shopify Integration Implementation Plan

## Overview

Create phased implementation plan with platform sequencing, dependencies, go/no-go checkpoints, and detailed timeline for Shopify ecosystem delivery.

## Prerequisites

- Architecture design document complete
- Platform selections confirmed with vendors
- Business requirements and priorities documented
- Stakeholder alignment on budget and timeline

## Steps

1. **Plan Kickoff & Review**: [step-01-init](steps/step-01-init.md)
2. **Dependency Mapping**: [step-02-dependencies](steps/step-02-dependencies.md)
3. **Sequencing & Phasing**: [step-03-sequencing](steps/step-03-sequencing.md)
4. **Go/No-Go Checkpoints**: [step-04-go-no-go](steps/step-04-go-no-go.md)
5. **Implementation Plan Document**: [step-05-plan](steps/step-05-plan.md)

## Completion Criteria

- All platforms sequenced with rationale
- Dependencies mapped and visualized
- Phases defined with clear objectives
- Go/no-go criteria established per phase
- Detailed timeline (Gantt chart) produced
- Risk register with mitigations
- Stakeholder sign-off on plan
