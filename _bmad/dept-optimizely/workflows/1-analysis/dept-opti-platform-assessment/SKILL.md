# Platform Assessment: CMS 12 vs SaaS CMS

**Entry Point**: `dept-opti-platform-assessment`

This skill assesses which Optimizely platform (CMS 12 PaaS or SaaS CMS) is the right fit for your project, also covering commerce integration and Opal AI opportunities.

## What This Skill Does

Evaluates your project requirements against both Optimizely platforms to answer:
- Which platform meets your needs best?
- What are the trade-offs?
- How does commerce integration work?
- Where do CMP and Opal AI fit?
- What's the total cost and effort?

## When To Use It

After source audit and ideally after content modelling starts. Use this skill to make the CMS 12 vs SaaS CMS decision that will drive architecture and implementation.

## Inputs

- Project requirements (business, technical, team, timeline)
- Current technology stack
- Content complexity from audit
- Team skills and capacity
- Budget and timeline constraints

## Outputs

- Platform Comparison Matrix (CMS 12 vs SaaS CMS across 10+ dimensions)
- Scoring and Weighted Recommendation
- Commerce Fit Analysis
- CMP and Opal AI Opportunity Assessment
- Risk Register and Mitigations
- High-Level Implementation Roadmap
- Final Platform Recommendation

## Process

The skill delegates to `workflow.md` which guides you through three steps:

1. **Initialize** - Gather requirements and constraints
2. **Evaluate** - Platform comparison and scoring
3. **Recommend** - Final recommendation with roadmap

Total time: 4-6 hours depending on complexity

## Success Criteria

✓ All project requirements documented
✓ Both platforms evaluated across 10+ dimensions
✓ Scoring complete and weighted
✓ Commerce and AI opportunities assessed
✓ Risk register built
✓ Roadmap outlined
✓ Final recommendation clear with rationale
✓ Stakeholders understand trade-offs and next steps
